About Us

We have four two-bedroom cottages, and a three bedroom house - all on a self catering basis and have been graded 4 stars by Visit Wales. The accommodation is within a courtyard of converted farm buildings, is well equipped, and has central heating for year-round comfort. Two of the cottages are classified as suitable for special needs with assistance. Being on a farm, we are surrounded by green fields, where the cattle and the horses graze. Children, especially those living in cities are always fascinated to be able to observe the animals in their natural habitat.

Many of the interesting features of Wales and the Capital city Cardiff can easily be visited. A wide choice of galleries and museums, theatres and cinemas and The Museum of Welsh Life at St Fagans as well as several well preserved castles are within easy reach.
